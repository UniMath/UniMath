Library UniMath.Bicategories.MonoidalCategories.ActionBasedStrongFunctorsMonoidal

shows that action-based strong functors can be perceived as strong monoidal functors from the monoidal category that is acting on the underlying categories to a suitable monoidal category
This means that the requirement on strength is that it behaves as a ``homomorphism'' w.r.t. the monoidal structures.
Work in progress: the characterization in the monoidal case will need a full development of displayed monoidal categories and their sections, which is why there is now only a construction of a strong monoidal functor from a parameterized distributivity and no construction in the other direction
Author: Ralph Matthes 2021
