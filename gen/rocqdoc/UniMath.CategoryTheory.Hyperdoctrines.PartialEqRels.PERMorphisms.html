2. Partial setoid morphisms

  Definition partial_setoid_morphism
    : UU
    := ‚àë (œÜ : form (X √óh Y)),

    : isaset partial_setoid_morphism.

    - intro.

3. Accessors for partial setoid morphisms

For each law we have a corresponding accessor. The accessors below are designed in such a way that they are more convenient to use. The substitutions are already calculated, and the necessary elimination rules for the universal quantifier and the implication are already instantiated. Finally, it is sufficiently weakened, so that one can apply them with an arbitrary context of assumptions.
  Proposition partial_setoid_mor_dom_defined

    fold x' y' in q.
    pose proof (hyperdoctrine_proof_subst (!! : tm Œì ùüô) q) as q'.
