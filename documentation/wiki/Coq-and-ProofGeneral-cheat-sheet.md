Coq Proof General supplies the following key-bindings:

    C-c C-a C-i
    Inserts “Intros ”

    C-c C-a C-a
    Inserts “Apply ”

    C-c C-a C-s
    Inserts “Section ”

    C-c C-a C-e
    Inserts “End <section-name>.” (this should work well with nested sections).

    C-c C-a C-o
    Prompts for a SearchIsos argument.

    C-c C-a C-n
    Locate "<notation>". 
    
    C-x v g
    Calls "git blame".
